                      THE DICTIONARY readme

1. To start the app just simply press on the app icon(open book with purple background).

2. First screen please choose the language you would like to learn.

3. Second screen please choose your primary language.

4. The third screen is your Dictionary(a place where you can see all added words)

5. If you'd like to add words just press on "+" in a circle at the bottom right corner.

6. To change words already added to your "Dictionary" just press on which you'd like to change.

7. In the top left corner by pressing the hamburger button you will see two settings you can press on. 
  The first one is to test your knowledge on vocabulary added to your "Dictionary" 
  The second one is to change the language you are learning(remember if you will proceed it will delete whole Dictionary data).
